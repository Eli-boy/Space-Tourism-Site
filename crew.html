FLIGHT ENGINEER

ANOUSHEH ANSARI

Anoussheh Ansari is an Iranian American engineer and co-

founder of Prodea Systems. Ansari was the fourth self-

funded space tourist, the self-funded woman to fly

to the ISS, and the first Iranian in space.
